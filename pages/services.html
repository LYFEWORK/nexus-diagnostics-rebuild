Diagnostic Services

Nexus Diagnostic Services

Three integrated solutions designed around the neurologist's workflow. From patient setup to report delivery, every step is built for clinical accuracy and physician convenience.

Primary Service

In-Home Video Ambulatory EEG

EEG Technicians set up patients in the comfort of their home for long-term studies. Our certified technicians handle the entire process — from electrode placement to equipment teardown — so the patient's routine is minimally disrupted.

Each study captures synchronized video, audio, and EEG data. Technicians remotely monitor data quality throughout, ensuring the recording meets clinical standards before the study concludes.

Video & Audio Capture

Synchronized recording provides complete clinical context for every event.

Intermittent & Continuous Monitoring

Choose the monitoring level your patient needs. Technicians verify data quality remotely.

Up to 7-Day Continuous Recording

Extended capture increases diagnostic yield for intermittent seizure events.

Daily Reporting Available

24-hour reporting intervals with full-study reports delivered upon completion.

Study Duration

3, 4, 5, or 7-day options (CPT: 95715)

Data Capture

Continuous EEG + video + audio

Monitoring

Intermittent or continuous technician monitoring

Reporting

Daily reports + non-destructive pruning

In-Practice Service

In-Office Routine EEG

Nexus technicians perform routine EEG studies directly in your practice. No equipment investment required — we bring everything, handle the setup, run the study, and deliver results through our interpreting platform.

Ideal for practices that need EEG capability without the overhead of purchasing and maintaining equipment or hiring dedicated EEG staff.

No Equipment Investment

We provide all hardware and handle calibration, setup, and maintenance.

Certified Technicians

Our EEG technicians are trained and certified to clinical standards.

Seamless Integration

Results delivered through the same web-based platform used for ambulatory studies.

Physician Platform

Web-Based Interpreting Software

Interpret your patients' EEGs remotely from any browser. No software installation required. Our platform is designed for neurologists who need fast, reliable access to study data from any location.

Browser-Based Access

No downloads, no installations. Open your browser and interpret.

Daily Reporting & Non-Destructive Pruning

24-hour daily reporting options. Pruned/clipped reports preserve raw data while delivering actionable summaries.

Remote Interpretation

Review studies from your office, home, or anywhere with an internet connection.
