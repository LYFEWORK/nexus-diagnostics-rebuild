How to Order an EEG Study

Ordering an ambulatory EEG study through Nexus is straightforward. Submit a referral with the required documentation, and we handle everything from insurance authorization to technician dispatch.

Submit Referral Call (561) 409-1483
Ordering Process

Three Steps to Order

Prepare Your Referral

Gather patient demographics, insurance information, diagnosis codes, clinical notes, and determine the study duration (3, 4, 5, or 7 days).

Submit Documentation

Fax the complete referral to (561) 258-8154 or submit it electronically through our referral form. Include the ordering physician's signature.

We Handle the Rest

Nexus verifies insurance, obtains authorization, schedules the study, and dispatches a certified technician to the patient's home.

Requirements

Electronic Orders Must Include

Patient demographics & insurance information
Duration of study (CPT: 95715) — 3, 4, 5, or 7-day options
Relevant diagnosis codes (e.g., G40.109, G40.209, R55, R42)
Previous EEG reports, if applicable
Clinical or encounter notes
Ordering physician's signature
